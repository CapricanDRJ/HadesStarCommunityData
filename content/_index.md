Welcome to the Hades' Star Community Data repository. This project aims to provide a comprehensive collection of data and resources for the Hades' Star video game. Here you can find the top corporations based on their Elo ratings, detailed match histories, and various resources for the Hades' Star community.

## Introduction

This project was created to help the Hades' Star community by providing valuable insights and data analytics. Whether you are a new player or a seasoned veteran, we hope you find this repository useful.

## Scoreboards

- [Top 100 Elo Ratings](top100.md) - The top 100 corporations ranked based on their performance in all White Star matches.
- [Top 100 Competitive Elo Ratings](comp100.md) - The competitive ranking excludes whichever scanner slot produces the lower score per 4 day period. 
- [Top 100 For Most Relics](relics100.md) - The leaderboard of corporations with the highest total of relics in any match.
- [Top 100 For Most WhiteStars](totalws.md) - The leaderboard of corporations with the highest total whitestars completed.
- [Top 100 Winning Corporations](win100.md) - The leaderboard of corporations with the highest total number of wins.
- [Top 100 Losing Corporations](lose100.md) - The leaderboard of corporations with the highest total number of Losses

## Corporations

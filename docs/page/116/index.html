Welcome to the Hades’ Star Community Data repository. This project aims to provide a comprehensive collection of data and resources for the Hades’ Star video game. Here you can find the top corporations based on their Elo ratings, detailed match histories, and various resources for the Hades’ Star community.

Introduction#

This project was created to help the Hades’ Star community by providing valuable insights and data analytics. Whether you are a new player or a seasoned veteran, we hope you find this repository useful.

Scoreboards#

  • Top 100 Elo Ratings - The top 100 corporations ranked based on their performance in all White Star matches.
  • Top 100 Competitive Elo Ratings - The competitive ranking excludes whichever scanner slot produces the lower score per 4 day period.
  • Top 100 For Most Relics - The leaderboard of corporations with the highest total of relics in any match.
  • Top 100 For Most WhiteStars - The leaderboard of corporations with the highest total whitestars completed.
  • Top 100 Winning Corporations - The leaderboard of corporations with the highest total number of wins.
  • Top 100 Losing Corporations - The leaderboard of corporations with the highest total number of Losses

Corporations#

  • Corporation Directory - Access detailed directories for each corporation, including match histories, scores, and other relevant data.

Resources#

  • White Star Matches (CSV) - CSV format of white star matches data.
  • White Star Matches (JSON) - JSON format of white star matches data.
  • White Star Matches (XLSX) - Excel format of white star matches data.
  • White Star Scoreboard (CSV) - CSV format of white star scoreboard.
  • White Star Scoreboard (JSON) - JSON format of white star scoreboard.
  • Red Star Bot Queue History (CSV) - Queue history from the Hades’ Star RS Q bot.
  • Hades Star Community Data GitHub - Link to the main GitHub repository.

Community Projects#

  • WS Community LeaderBoard Discord - Discord invite for the whitestar community leaderboard
  • Hades Space - A community resource site for Hades’ Star
  • Red Star Queue Bot - Discord bot invite for the Hades’ Star Red Star Queue
  • DRS Mining Simulator - CapSora’s dark red star mining simulator
  • KestraAkadi’s Youtube Channel - The Star League Hades’ Star corporation whitestar replay records.
  • Hades’ Star ELO: Recalculates and updates ELO scores every 15 minutes.
  • Hades’ Star RS Q: Manages Red Star queues with button interactions.
  • Hades’ Star Trader: Manages trading lists and inventory for artifacts.
  • Hades’ Star Tech: Streamlines tech sharing and alt management.
